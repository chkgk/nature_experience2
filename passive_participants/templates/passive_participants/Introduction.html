Thank you for participating.
If you read these rules carefully and choose wisely, you can earn up to US$ 3.50 by participating in a game that involves other participants.

Completing this task will take about 7 minutes and it is important that you pay close attention.

The task involves two participants: An active participant who makes decisions and a passive participant who does not make decisions. Both participants receive payments that are determined by the active participant’s decision and by chance. The task has four stages:

First, active participants take a small quiz about the game.

Then active participants are matched with a co-player, which is a computer, and the first round of the game is played.

Thereafter, active participants are matched anew with a computer co-player and the second round of the game is played.

Finally, all participants are asked to fill out a short survey.

Note that only one of the two rounds will be selected for payment at random.

Payments earned by the computer co-player go to the passive participant who is not taking any active decision in the game.

Remember: Your payoff is jointly determined by:

  • whether a red or a green ball is drawn from an urn containing 20 red and 80 green balls,
  • whether your action is A or B, and
  • whether your co-player's action is A or B.

The co-playersâ€™ action is programmed to be A in about 60 out of 100 cases and B in about 40 out of 100 cases.

If a green ball is drawn, and:

  • your action is A, and your co-player chooses A: You receive US$ 1. The passive participant you are matched with receives US$ 1.
  • your action is A, and your co-player chooses B: You receive US$ 1. The passive participant you are matched with receives US$ 3.
  • your action is B, and your co-player chooses A: You receive US$ 3. The passive participant you are matched with receives US$ 1.
  • your action is B, and your co-player chooses B: You receive nothing (US$ 0). The passive participant you are matched with receives nothing (US$ 0).

If a red ball is drawn, you receive nothing (US$ 0). The passive participant you are matched with receive nothing (US$ 0).
